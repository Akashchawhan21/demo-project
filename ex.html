Campus

Our campus offers a peaceful and inspiring environment for learning, growth, and development. Surrounded by greenery, it provides students with the perfect balance of academics and recreation. The well-maintained classrooms, modern laboratories, spacious library, and digital facilities ensure quality education, while the playgrounds, cultural spaces, and activity centers encourage students to explore their talents beyond academics. The campus also has dedicated spaces for relaxation and interaction, making it a vibrant place where students feel at home

Computer Lab

Equipped with modern computers, high-speed internet, and advanced software, our computer lab empowers students to develop technical skills and work on real-world projects in a practical environment.

Library

Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge with thousands of books, journals, and e-resources to support students learning and research. It offers a quiet, comfortable environment perfect for study and exploration.

Play Ground

Our spacious playground encourages sportsmanship, teamwork, and fitness. It offers facilities for multiple sports including cricket, football, and athletics to promote a healthy lifestyle.
